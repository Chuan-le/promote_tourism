customer's review

The amusement park used to be elaborately invested and built is the highlight of the park. However, over the years, this place is only ruins with mossy walls. According to the review of Ho Thuy Tien Hue by previous visitors, you can try to set foot inside the aquarium. Here, there are only fish tanks that are not intact, the walls are covered with paint, making the space more ghostly and wild.

Chuẩn lê

The way to Hue Thuy Tien Lake is Thien An hill road because this park is located right on the hill. Therefore, when coming to Thuy Tien Lake, visitors can take advantage of the beautiful scenery of Thien An hill. This is a fairly large hilly area, covered with tall pines. This place is currently a very attractive camping and photography spot for tourists.

Chuẩn lê

From Hue Thuy Tien Lake to Vong Canh hill is quite close, so when you come to this amusement park, don't forget to stop by this famous hill. Vong Canh Hill has long been known as the most beautiful place to see Hue from above. Coming here, tourists will admire firsthand the most beautiful bend of the Perfume River and have a comfortable relaxing time while walking under the soaring pines.
